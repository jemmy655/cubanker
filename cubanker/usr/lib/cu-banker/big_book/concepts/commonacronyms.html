Common Acronyms

The use of acronyms is universally annoying, so to demystify these irritating necessities the following tables are provided:

Banking Acronyms

AcronymMeaning
EFTElectronic Funds Transfer
EMVEuroPay, MasterCard, Visa - chip card standards
FICAFinancial Intelligence Centre Act - South African KYC regulations

POSPoint of Sale - a card accepting device also called a terminal or pinpad
NCANational Credit Act - South African legislation aimed at preventing reckless lending
ACHAutomated Clearing House - usually for EFTs
PCHPayment Clearing House - usually focused on a specific payment type eg. ATM PCH

CRCredit
